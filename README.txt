Rolls posts of a particular category on a position marked with [kiosk_slides]

== Description ==

Kiosk slides shows posts of a particular category as carrousel sliding from right to left on post after another.

You can position this slides in your content by using the placeholder [kiosk_slides].

== Installation ==

1. Unzip 'enformio_kiosk_slides.zip' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Setup this slug of the post category and the default interval on the settings page

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does 'Kiosk slides' call home =

No, 'Kiosk slides' just runs in your instance.

= My posts don't show up! Why? =

You need to place '[kiosk_slides]' in your content at the spot where you want it to show up.
